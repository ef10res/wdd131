Site Name: Davis County Soccer League

Site Purpose:

This site provides clients with the ability to register for the Davis County
soccer League. It includes general information about the league like location and office
It also includes a position table for rankings and top goal scorers and assist leaders.

Scenarios:

When does the season start and when should I register by? Are the soccer fields big enough for 11 vs 11?

Color Schema:

I will use the three primary colors as my schema.
They represent my favorite soccer team and white will also be present. blue will go in the header and footer. red in the headings and yellow in the header and footer font.

Typography:

I will use the Roboto font on headings and some table information and the work sans in the paragraphs and navigation.
